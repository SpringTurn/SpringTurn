【教程】申请文书模范合集 01期

前言

在上一次的推文中，大家是不是对Personal Statement的 写作技巧 还没有看过瘾？今天，！这次绝对是干货超级大放送！

文书是传递向招生官表达你自己的媒介，相当于一次文字面试，让招生官深刻了解你的个性、品德、学识及语言能力。因此，除了对内容的雕刻以外，你还要注意语言的准确性。让我们一起来看看出自春藤老师们是如何把PS的内容和语言拿捏得如此准确的吧！

科目

01 Business 商科

02 Humanities 人文

03 Law 法律

04 Politics 政治

05 Science 理工

06 附录 部分英美高校的PS要求

商科

低分文书

Designed to train aspiring Chartered Certified Accountants, my undergraduate ACCA program has not only equipped me with a solid foundation in business, which can be exemplified by my 90+ grades in advanced courses in finance and accounting, but also cultivated my strategic thinking and analytic skills. I once took the course of Operation Capital Management and led a team to complete a research. We analyzed financials of two famous snack food companies so as to explore which is the reasonable behavior of enterprise transformation decision, diversification strategy or merger and acquisition. Such practice on the firm-level and external factors that influence decisions made in financial management charm me and will augment my professional abilities.

模范文书

I believe my academic background has provided me with a solid base to commence the MSc. Graduating from an undergraduate ACCA programme with top grades in advanced courses in finance and accounting such as Financial Management(89/100) and Management Accounting(100/100) demonstrates that I have the potential to excel in an academically rigorous environ-ment. Studying core modules such as Audit and Assurance developed my strategic thinking and analytical skills. As part of the core ‘Working Capital Management’ core module I wrote a report evaluating company performance after a period of strategic change. Qiaqia Food and Haoxiangni Food are two food enterprises which adopted very different business strategies in the face of the 2012 financial crisis. I analysed the annual report of each entity, subsequent to their respective IPOs and evaluated their financial performance. From this I concluded that M&A is a better strategy for growth rather than diversification for companies in food industry. A strong awareness of accounting is essential to understand and assess a firm’s true financial performance and evaluate the impact of different business strategies.

（伦敦政治经济学院 会计硕士）

点评

在开头亮出自己优异的学术背景及相关课程来展示自己的学习潜能。然后列举在课程中学习到的实际案例，并描述自己所做的调查与分析，进而得出自己在分析事物时时刻具备会计意识的结论。首句中心句、中段举例论证、结尾总结，完美的表达了申请者在该学科的能力。

人文

低分文书

I was born in Xian. Xi’an is a place with more than 2000 years of history and it was also the start of silk road in Han Dynasty. I began to show interest in ancient culture because of Xi’an’s influence. When I was young I really loved to know about history and archaeology, I kept my dream when I grew up and even today I love them. The love for history and archaeology guided me to become an interesting student. The great buildings and cities I see everyday reminds me of the history and I became eager to learn about how complex societies were formed with different ways.

模范文书

I was born in Xi’an, whose history exceeds 2000 years thus exhibiting its remarkable historic value. Just as Xi’an marked the starting point of the Silk Road since at least Western Han Dynasty, it also marks the starting point of my interest in the remarkable field of ancient civilizations. My enthusiasm for both history and archaeology emerged when I was still a child and that enthusiasm has been kept alive throughout the years and has guided me to develop my own academic interests. The majestic monuments and settlements we can still see today fascinate me and thus I am eager to seek understanding of how such complex societies were constructed following different trajectories.

（牛津大学 考古学硕士）

点评

介绍自己生活背景是如何与人文学科相关的，一语双关点明自己出生的城市不仅是历史之乡，还是自己对申请专业感兴趣的开始。结尾总结学习该学科与社会的关联性，进一步道出自己对学习该学科的强烈愿望。别出心裁的故事和多样性的用词抓住了招生官的注意力，让他们好奇接下来PS里会发生什么有意思的故事。

法律

低分文书

I am fully prepared for taking further academic challenges with my strong learning initiative, enthusiasm for law and previous experiences. I believe that these qualities will allow me to keep pace with, and compete with my fellow students.

模范文书

The changes that Big Data and AI have brought to humanity as well our global society are redefining the relationship between enterprises, customers, employers and employees. Have regulators fully prepared to respond to social relations alternation? Have they also thought of ways to balance the conflict between traditional manufacturing industry and innovative opportunities? I must say that I am excited about the challenges ahead, and have faith in my strong learning initiative, enthusiasm and previous experiences. Upon graduation, my short-term plan would be to find job opportunities in the cyber security and intellectual property spheres of State or top companies such as Google, Apple, Alibaba, and Huawei. I would also like to cooperate with experts in the fields of Cyber Security, Information Technology, Computer Science or related fields. In the long term, I wish to ultimately pursue my research on Cyber Law and Technology Regulation, as well as to conduct more systematic, comprehensive, in-depth, and insightful research in academia.

（牛津大学 考古学硕士）

点评

在段首写出自己感兴趣的范围对当今社会的影响，并连续提出两个问题来吸引招生官兴趣，然后娓娓道出自己对这些挑战充满期待和热情。在后半部分写出毕业后的短期和长期目标，让面试官明白你是一个有计划、有理想的人，并让他们能够明确地了解你未来的发展是否和他们的教学理念相同。

政治

低分文书

After four years of my undergraduate study life, I realized that the most effective way of studying Chinese politics would require not only an inside story from China but also an outside image from the UK. Therefore, I decided to apply for a master program to study politics in a UK university, to learn and understand the British view of China. In order to dive into my studying, I selected the LSE—a prestigious UK university which has educated numerous political leaders of many countries, as my first choice in order to further develop my understanding of political science as a whole and Chinese politics in particular.

模范文书

I believe that under the guidance of the professors mentioned above and many other professors in related fields, I will be able to conduct a deeper research on Chinese politics during my master’s program. My ultimate goal is to obtain a doctoral degree related to Chinese politics and to seek a faculty position to continue my academic research. However, my academic dream cannot be realized without the strict training and guidance from the professors at LSE. At the same time, I also believe that my cross-cultural background and deep underst-anding of China can bring a new perspective to teachers and students in class discussions, and even offer some help to professors in relevant research projects.

（牛津大学 考古学硕士）

点评

此段落深刻地展示了申请人对目标院校政治专业深入的了解，通过总结前文提到的该专业的授课教授，让招生官知道申请人为这门专业做足了功课。之后，道出自己未来的目标，并表示没有目标院校教授的指导是无法完成的，来获取招生官好感。除了学生自己的能力，学校更看重的是学生能给学校这个community带来什么，这一段很好的说明了申请人和其他学生的不同之处，那就是能利用自己多元文化的背景和对该学科独特及深入的认识，来帮同学、老师及教授。

理工

低分文书

Once I worked in Wilmar Oleo (Shanghai) Co. LTD, and I met a lot of friends and learnt many things there. At that time, I felt very happy. I proposed many suggestions to my manager, and he thanked me a lot and inspired me to do new things. That experience in the workshop make me believe that I can do well in my future career.

模范文书

Moreover, I also gained a lot during my internship. In the summer vacation of 2018, I took an internship in Wilmar Oleo (Shanghai) Co. LTD. At that time, the soap-grain hydrops in soap-grain workshop were very serious. I proposed setting the horizontal pipe into a stepped dry pipe, which was approved later. The manager spoke highly of me during the internship. This experience together with the manager’s recog-nition made me firmly believe that I want to become an engineer in the material engineering industry in the future.

（牛津大学 考古学硕士）

点评

详细介绍自己相关的实习经历，来表达自己在该专业的实践。与其他文书不同的是，申请者在此段中描述了自己在实习中遇到的问题，并提出解决方案，最后得到上级肯定的故事。最后总结根据此实习经历，申请者更加确信自己要进一步探索此领域。通过描述解决棘手的问题，让招生官看到申请者除了拥有书本上的知识，还能活学活用。

名校文书要求

哥伦比亚大学

所有申请人必须提交一篇学术目标陈述。请组织1000字左右的陈述描述：

1. 你过往与所申请方向相关的学术经历和准备工作；

2. 你在哥伦比亚大学进行研究生学习的计划以及之后的职业目标；

3. 如果你准备离开其他学校的研究生项目，请解释你作出此决定的原因；

4. 如果你目前没有就读于一个学术项目，请描述自你最近一次在一个学术机构入学至今的活动情况；

5. 如果在你的教育经历中有过间隔，请附上简短的解释。

伦敦政经学院

你的个人陈述不应长于两页A4纸，字数在1000-1500字之间。在文书中我们希望看到以下内容：

1. 申请者申请此项目的动机；

2. 与本项目相关的学术兴趣、优势和背景；

3. 与本项目相关的学术志向或研究兴趣；

4. 职业理想，以及项目的学术训练如何能够帮助你的职业发展；

5. 其他相关信息，比如你做过的课外阅读、研究或工作经历如何启发你申请本项目。
